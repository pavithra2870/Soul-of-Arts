Our Story

Art Is Not What
You See, But What
You Make Others See

Soul of Arts was born from a passion to make fine art accessible to everyone. We believe every person carries an innate creative spark — our role is to fan that flame. Through guided classes, personal mentorship, and an inspiring studio environment, we help artists of every age discover their unique voice.

What We Offer

Our Classes

🖌️

Acrylic & Oil Painting

Master colour theory, brush techniques, layering, and texture with acrylic and oil paints. From still life to portrait to landscape — a complete painting education.

💧

Watercolour Art

Explore the flowing, transparent beauty of watercolours. Learn wet-on-wet, dry brush, and wash techniques for stunning landscapes and florals.

✏️

Pencil Sketching & Drawing

Build your foundational drawing skills — shading, proportion, perspective, and figure drawing. A strong sketch is the backbone of every great artwork.

🌀

Zentangle & Pattern Art

Discover the meditative power of Zentangle — structured patterns, repetitive strokes, and intricate line work that result in breathtaking illustrations.

🎨

Kids Art Program

Fun, structured, and imagination-first classes for young learners. Covering basic drawing, colouring, and creative expression through varied art projects every week.

🏛️

Indian Traditional Art

Celebrate India's rich artistic heritage — from mythological paintings and devotional art to folk-inspired patterns and vibrant cultural storytelling.
